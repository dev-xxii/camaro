The 1960s saw the debut of many powerful and high-performance sports cars such as the Ford Mustang, Dodge Charger, and Challenger. Among these muscle cars, the Chevrolet Camaro made quite a name. Besides, Chevrolet Camaro is among the last breeds of the American muscle car era.

Engine Ability

Chevrolet Camaro came with a choice of eight engines in its first generation. Later, a special performance edition was included featuring a V8 engine.

Performance

Performance figures include a 0 to 60 mph (0 to 97 km/h) acceleration time of 3.5 seconds.

Cutting Edge

The Z/28 came with a 302 CID V8 engine which was produced to meet the race legalities of that time.

  • Maximum Torque 617 Nm (455 lb.ft) at 4600 rpm
  • Turning Radius 11.7 m / 38.4 feet
  • No. of cylinders V 8
  • Drive Type RWD
